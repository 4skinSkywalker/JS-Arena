Your task is to write a query that finds the recommendation chain starting from the member with ID 7. The result should list each member in this chain with their ID, first name, and surname, ordered by descending member ID.

Structure

The member information is stored in the following way:

  • memid: Unique identifier for the member.
  • surname: The member's last name.
  • firstname: The member's first name.
  • recommendedby: ID of the member who recommended them.

Example

Given the following members table:

memid surname firstname recommendedby
1 Smith John null
2 Doe Jane 1
3 Brown Michael 1
4 Johnson Emily 2
5 Williams Daniel 2
6 Taylor Sarah 3
7 Anderson James 5
8 Thomas Olivia 4
9 Jackson David null
10 White Sophia 9

Output

The expected result for member ID 7 would look like this:

memid firstname surname
7 Anderson James
5 Daniel Williams
2 Jane Doe
1 John Smith
